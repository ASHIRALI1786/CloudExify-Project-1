SALES DATA ANALYSIS REPORT

Project:
Sales Data Analysis

Objective:
The objective of this project was to analyze a retail sales dataset using Python and generate business insights through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).

Tasks Performed:
1. Loaded the dataset.
2. Cleaned missing values.
3. Removed duplicate records.
4. Converted date columns into datetime format.
5. Performed descriptive statistical analysis.
6. Identified top-selling products.
7. Analyzed category-wise sales.
8. Analyzed region-wise sales.
9. Created charts using Matplotlib.
10. Generated business insights.

Business Insights:
- Top products contribute significantly to total sales.
- Sales vary across different regions.
- Categories perform differently in terms of revenue.
- Monthly sales show business trends.
- Profit analysis helps identify the most profitable categories.

Conclusion:
This project demonstrates the use of Python, Pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib for cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing sales data to support business decision-making.
